WHO IS JON?

As a fulltime freelance musician based in Surrey, UK, Jon divides his time as a wedding musician, guitar tutor, session musician and singer-songwriter. His professional musicianship has taken him all over the world including USA and Belgium, and even performing on ABC Radio. Jon's fingerstyle/singer-songwriter material has received high praise from previous customers, other musicians and the music industry. He has been featured in Acoustic Magazine's 'The next big thing' and has played the prestigious 'London Acoustic Guitar Show'. 

David Sinclair (a pop & rock critic for The Times) said his solo material "Raises the bar to impressive heights". He has incorporated this style in all areas of his music business. Check out www.jonhartmusic.com for bio, audio, photos, video, press and more. 

WHAT'S UNIQUE ABOUT JON?

Imagine the sound of a live acoustic duo/trio played solo. Jon Hart is an acoustic wedding guitarist and wedding singer that incorporates vocal harmony, fingerstyle guitar and the occasional beatboxing to add a unique twist to those classic covers from all eras (from The Beatles to Ed Sheeran).

With a high energy solo performance and experience that spans over a decade, he leaves audiences craving more. By using specialist equipment (no backing tracks), Jon thickens the sound by using a vocal harmony pedal and looping pedal for beat boxing. This gives the impression there are many musicians, or even a small band playing. 

The big bonus is, this setup is small and can fit into all venues, but can still create a sound that is powerful or intimate.
